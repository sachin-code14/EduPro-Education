 EduPro Student Enrollment & Demographic Insights
1- Project Overview

EduPro Student Enrollment & Demographic Insights is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze student enrollment patterns, demographic distribution, and course preferences on an online learning platform. The dashboard transforms enrollment data into actionable insights, helping educational institutions understand learner demographics, course popularity, and enrollment trends.

By combining key performance indicators (KPIs), interactive visualizations, and demographic analysis, the dashboard supports data-driven decisions for improving course offerings, student engagement, and academic planning.

2- Business Problem

Educational institutions and online learning platforms often manage thousands of student enrollments across different courses and learning levels. Without proper analytics, it becomes difficult to answer questions such as:

Which courses attract the highest number of students?
What is the distribution of learners by age and gender?
Which course levels are most popular?
What percentage of students choose free versus paid courses?
How do enrollment patterns vary across demographic groups?

This dashboard provides a centralized view of these metrics to support strategic planning and improve learning outcomes.

5- Key Insights

-The platform has a balanced gender distribution with a slight majority of female learners.
-The 18–25 age group represents the largest share of enrolled students.
-Free courses account for the majority of enrollments, indicating a strong preference for accessible learning.
-Beginner-level courses receive the highest enrollment, reflecting demand from new learners.
-Course categories such as Data Science, Web Development, and Artificial Intelligence attract significant student interest.
-Enrollment patterns vary across age groups, helping identify target audiences for different course offerings.
